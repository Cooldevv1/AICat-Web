AICat Privacy Policy

Welcome! Your privacy means a lot to us. This page should *hopefully* explain how we are collecting and using your data.

1.

Knowing what Discord servers you are in

When you authenticate with Discord, we will automatically find out precisely what servers you are in at the time of authentication. We only use this data as we require all users to be in the CatWeb Discord server.

2.

Permission to join Discord servers on your behalf

We only request the ability to join Discord servers on your behalf as we require all users to be in the official AICat Discord server. This allows our staff to much more easily improve your experience and to help us better moderate our platform.

3.

Conversations with AICat

All conversations are stored and logged to better help moderate our platform. You can delete this data on your account. AICat uses OpenRouter for AI usage so your usage is also subject to their terms. (https://openrouter.ai)

4.

Limitation of Liability

We are not liable for any data loss as highlighted in our Terms of Service.

5.

GDPR and EU Digital Services Act

You can request your data to be deleted from our Services on our Website or by creating a ticket on our Discord server.
